Cars

1-What is a car?

A car is a four wheeled metal that has an engine to rotate the wheels and equiped with a lot of other components such as, suspension, brakes, tail lights, head lights...

2-Components of a regular car

The regular is composed from plenty: engine, suspension, exhaust, suspension, brakes, wheels, body, transmission, etc......

3-Tuning cars

Tuning is the best thing you can do a 1990's cars and new ones as well! Tuning is uprading the car by upgrading the engine and adding some extra pieces and components, the famous tuning is adding big turbos or twin turbos, antilag, backfire, superchargers, modded carburator...., to reach high numbers of horsepower and torque.

4-Cars vs Motorcycles

For me cars are better than motorcycles because cars are the best in tuning, also, cars are the only ones who have the best tutbo sound stutututututu, the best antilag, and the best backfire. Cars come stock (standard) from companies, their tuning is very very satisfying, you can moddify the body, the chassis, and the engine, but for motorcycles, you just buy them stock from companies, you can tune them too, but not like cars.
